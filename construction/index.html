Construction

Quality work. Honest service. Great Value.

Heidrea's full-service construction division prides itself in providing over 20 years' experience in design and installation to perform high-quality work on any size project. Each customer is provided a dedicated project manager who will be in constant contact on the status of the project. Furthermore, Heidrea has the advantage of utilizing its in-house electrical division, which ensures completion of projects with minimal use of outside contractors. Our combination of experienced personnel using state-of-the-art design software and innovative equipment ensures a high-quality end-product as well as a highly satisfied customer.

+ Excavation

Fully licensed and over 20 years of experience in the excavation field our operators can achieve success in the most challenging environments. Foundations, electrical, water, and gas line trenching, new lawns, grade changing, driveway installs, concrete flatwork, dump truck services, small crane services and land clearing are a few of the services we can provide you.

+ Carpentry

Our crews and crew leaders are professional and courteous and work with the client throughout the entire project. Finish carpentry, Kitchen remodels, additions, new construction, bathroom remodels, tile installation, flooring, and decks are a few of the services we can provide. Innovative thinking and attention to detail always leaves our clients impressed with our finished product.

+ Demolition

Keeping a neat and organized job site is key to a successful demolition job. With our equipment and work ethic we can handle a wide variety of jobs from residential in home demo to large scale concrete removal. We have the right tools and the experience to accomplish all jobs in a neat and orderly fashion.

+ Digitally Designed Hardscapes

Using the industry-leading software PRO Landscape we are able to give you a product the helps you visualize the final product before we break ground. Clients are able to make any design changes before the product is installed. Heidrea can design and install your outdoor living spaces including custom concrete tables, counter tops, water features, fire places, patios, walkways, home landscapes and outdoor kitchens.
