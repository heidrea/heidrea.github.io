APPLY



Tower Technicians

Tower Technicians, also referred to as climbers, work as a team to install, maintain, repair and modify a variety of wireless equipment on infrastructures. Tower Technicians must be in good physical condition, and enjoy climbing with zero fear of heights.

Installer Technicians

Installer technicians work in all kinds of environments and are responsible for installing and commissioning DC Power and Battery plants as well as central office / MTSO telecom equipment. Installers clean, test, splice and connect fiber optic cables and terminate various types of cables such as twisted pair copper, coaxial, power, fiber, CAT5/CAT6 & RG8 cabling. Electrical experience is preferred for this position.

Civil Laborers

Civil Laborers perform a variety of construction related duties such carpentry, demolition, excavation concrete and more! General construction and labor experience is preferred.

Electricians

Heidrea's electrical division works to install low voltage and D.C cabling in both the telecommunication and electrical industry. Wiring and installations are performed on residential, commercial and industrial facilities. Heidrea is always looking to hire Journeyman and Apprentice Electricians with preferred experience to join our team!
