About Us

Heidrea Communications is a veteran-owned, limited liability company incorporated in the state of Massachusetts since 2008. While we are a general contracting firm our primary focus is on building and maintaining wireless communications towers.

Our Founders

Jesse G. Brown

Co-Founder

Jesse designs, develops, communicates, and executes the strategic goals of the company in support of his vision to meet all customer needs in every aspect of the wireless construction market. Jesse leads the company by overseeing the day-to-day operations, including; project management, estimation and bidding for jobs, finances, job scheduling, material purchases and safety.

As a Marine Corps Veteran, Jesse maintains strong partnerships in the Veteran community through Heidrea For Heroes. Jesse is sensitive to the day to day hardships endured by Veterans injured in the line of service. He is personally dedicated to provide Veterans and their families the support needed through local services, specialized equipment, and individual projects. His commitment to the success of his foundation will serve the Veteran community for years to come.

Matthew Mastroianni

Co-Founder

Matthew has extensive experience in the wireless industry, having held leadership roles in wireless companies for over six years. Matthew oversees a field staff of over 50 employees, where he successfully ensures jobs are completed timely, within budget, with a focus on safety protocols. He also manages a fleet of company vehicles and warehouse inventories.

Matthew is a Marine Corps Veteran who continuously gives back to those in his community and throughout the state of Massachusetts who have served in the military. Heidrea For Heroes is the perfect avenue for his ongoing commitment to assisting Veterans.

Community

Jesse Brown and Matthew Mastroianni are the co-founders and current board members of Heidrea for Heroes. H4H is a charitable organization, whose mission is to provide adaptive housing and transportation, Veteran/family support, and specialized equipment that will inspire and motivate veterans and families to move forward in a positive direction during unexpected hardships as a result of the unselfish acts of our United States Veterans.

To learn more about the efforts of this organization, please visit its website www.heidrea4heroes.org.

Bellingham company specializes in tower construction

It's only seems appropriate that Heidrea Communications LLC has incorporated the Marine Corps' motto of semper fi - always faithful - into its operation. That's because its two founders, Jesse Brown and Matt Mastroianni are both Marine Corps veterans and also because more than 30 percent of the company's 38 employees are U.S. veterans. Its work has not gone unnoticed.

The Bellingham company, a general contracting firm specializing in the wireless industry and focusing on tower construction and maintenance, recently received the Massachusetts Small Business Administration (SBA) Veteran-Owned Small Business of the Year Award for 2013 from the U.S. SBA.

The selection for this award was based on staying power, growth in number of employees, increase in sales and/or unit volume, current and past financial reports, innovation of product or service, and response to adversity and community contributions. Heidrea Communications was nominated for the award by Anne Trevisani, a vice president and commercial loan officer for South Eastern Economic Development Corp. in Taunton.
